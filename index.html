Experience and Reliability

Located in Macon, GA. Macon Metal Works is a small fabrication shop that specializes in custom designs, small production runs, one off projects and turning your vision into a reality. Combined we have decades of experience in Metal Fabrication, CNC Welding, MIG/Tig Welding, AutoCAD Design, Business Signage, Metal Wall Art, 3D Sculptures, Fire Pits, Custom Automotive Parts, and Displays

High Quality - Made in USA

The products we sell are products we feel confident using ourselves. Period. We do not cut corners and would never produce an item we were not confident in. We use American Made Materials whenever possible and are proud to produce all of products locally in Georgia.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

We are two childhood friends trying to make an honest living while chasing our dreams of entrepreneurship. You'll be dealing with one of us, not a pushy un-informed salesperson. If you are not happy, we will make it right. Our name, reputation and future is dependent on your satisfaction.
